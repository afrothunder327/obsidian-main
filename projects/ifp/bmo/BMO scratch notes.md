The following note contains various clippings and notes on the US Department of Defense [Biotechnology Management Office](https://www.govinfo.gov/content/pkg/BILLS-119hr6664ih/pdf/BILLS-119hr6664ih.pdf).

#### Core mandate
- The office will "foster the development, acquisition, and sustainment of broad-based biotechnology capabilities of the Department."
- Key sub-responsibilities of interest:
	- Maintain and execute a long-term research, development, acquisition and sustainment roadmap.
	- Updating policies and guidance on biotechnology products.
	- Coordination with other actors on biotechnology.
	- Proposing options for streamlining regulatory and acquisitions process for the department.
	- Identifying and filling biotechnology workforce gaps.

The BIOSECURE Act also included in the NDAA directs the BMO to publish an initial list of biotechnology companies of concern within a year. **How are they thinking about what is a company of concern?**
