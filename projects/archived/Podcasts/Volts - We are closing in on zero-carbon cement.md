To follow up, I think you need to:
- Check that there aren't other promising technologies.
- Find sources backing up Leah and David's claims that CO2 injection, CCS, and other process are not really that exciting.
- Read up a little more on these standards.
- Your case study should also look into policy landscape a little. In the cement world it sounds like some policies might make some companies quite happy to pay green premiums, in which case you might not need demand demonstrations. This mostly sounded like the EU.
- For each technology you need to have some sense of who the biggest buyers are. For instance 60% of cement in the US is purchased by government, so demonstration of government demand is critical.
