# A Pipeline Runs Through It

## Reflection
#### Summary
I found the ending on how the colonial scramble for oil was a large contributor to the political entanglements that culminated in a large scale world war. The early development of the oil industry in Pennsylvania was also interesting, but not quite as compelling as the development of the industry in Russia.

I enjoyed the author's perspective. Although the book ultimately drifted toward a focus on the British experience it provided a balanced portrait of the non Western actors in the Middle East.
