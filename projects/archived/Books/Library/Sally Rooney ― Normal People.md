# Normal People

## Reflection
#### Summary
I think it started very well. Rooney's characters and their emotional dramas are extremely relatable, and she has a way of describing them with simple clarity. I can't find the right expression here, but her description are quite plain but very precise.

The characters lost me as the story progressed --- they seemed to lack development both in themselves and in their relationship. Overall I had fun reading a book merely for pleasure.
