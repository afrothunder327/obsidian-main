# Under the Net

## Reflection
#### Summary
Big themes here worth reflecting on later:
- Truth is speech
- Is all communication persuasion?
- I think there was something about how well one can know oneself. The protagonist has these rich narratives of themselves and their actions, but they often flip flop on things they said earlier. This is another way in which are under the net, although I'm not sure that I agree with the title.
- Finn, the speechless man, has perhaps the greatest intuition.
- Loved the description of Mrs Tinkham. The chain smoking image may never leave me!

This is hardly unpleasant. You should write book reviews after everything you read!
