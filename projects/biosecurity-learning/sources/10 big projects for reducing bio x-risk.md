## Notes

Ideas I'm most interested in.

1. Ensure PPE stockpiles are in place in country X. They want this for >500 million people by the end of 2027. Wow.
2. Standing AI-Bio demo team.
3. Credible restriction on mirror life in country X by end of 2028.
4. Rigorously tested DIY protocols for cleanrooms and respirators.
5. *Scalably monitor particle concentrations inside clean spaces and respirators.* This might be a great fit for the military to pilot. Or just for a pull mechanism more broadly.
	1. Existing particle monitors cost thousands of dollars, aren't designed for stockpiling or in-respirator wear, and have no manufacturing plan suited for crisis ramp-up.
	2. This could be pull-shaped. But risk of getting MSA involved is that they won't be truth-seeking.
